NINTENDO VS SEGA

When I was around 7 years old I got my first game console. A Sega Genesis. I spent a long time building up a huge collection of games for it until I was around 10. When I was 10 my brother convinced me that the Super Nintendo was the thing to own, and that I should trade all my stuff into Funcoland to get one. I traded all 50 or so games that I had in and got a Super Nintendo with one game. The game was called Starfox and was very fun to play. Over the next few years I spent all my allowance and eventual job money building up my Super Nintendo collection. I still can't figure out which one I really liked better.
